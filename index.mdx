Semoia (Beta) is an **Intellectual Ledger** that helps you capture, organize, and reuse knowledge from your AI chat sessions sustainably. 

By leveraging the **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**, Semoia connects directly to your favorite AI tools like Cursor, Windsurf, and Claude, allowing them to access your personalized knowledge base in real-time.
