## Get started in two steps

### 1. Browser Extension

The Extension is the bridge between Semoia and AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.).

<Card
  title="Download Extension"
  icon="download"
  href="https://chrome.google.com/webstore"
>
  Download the Semoia Extension from Chrome Web Store (link coming soon).
</Card>

<Steps>
<Step title="Download Extension from Chrome Web Store">
  Download and install the Semoia Extension from Chrome Web Store to get started.
</Step>

<Step title="Pin Extension to browser toolbar">
  After installation, pin the Extension to your browser toolbar for easy access.
</Step>

<Step title="Sign in to sync with your Web account">
  Sign in to the Extension to sync with your Web account.
</Step>

<Step title="Capture conversation">
  When chatting with AI, click the Semoia icon to "Capture" (collect) the conversation into the queue.
</Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Access Dashboard

Access the Dashboard to manage your Ledgers.

Sign in via Clerk (Google/GitHub/Email) at [Dashboard](https://www.trysemoia.com).

<Card
  title="Open Dashboard"
  icon="window"
  href="https://www.trysemoia.com"
>
  Access the Dashboard to start managing your Ledgers and Memories.
</Card>
